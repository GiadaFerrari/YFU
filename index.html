BEHIND THE SCENES

To travel is to live

BEHIND THE SCENES is a YFU campaign that gives you a never seen before real life window on what a travel experience with us would mean. Each year we appoint a representer that can capture the best (and worst) moments of an exchange with YFU, all through an Instagram profile which functions as a diary for you to look through.

Our representer will be free to post real life experiences, thoughts, videos and quotes that best represents both the beauty and the struggle of living and growing in a YFU experience.

Explore our destinations, look at the different struggles and achievement each country can give you and be inspired to travel beyond your dreams through our ambassadors eyes!
